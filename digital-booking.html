Digital Booking System

A modern booking platform designed to automate appointments, manage customers, and simplify business operations through a secure and scalable digital system.

The Challenge

Many small businesses still rely on phone calls, spreadsheets, and messaging applications to manage appointments. This often creates scheduling conflicts, missed bookings, double reservations, and unnecessary administrative work. The goal of this project was to build a reliable digital solution that automates booking management while providing a better experience for both businesses and customers.

Core Features

Appointment Management

Create, edit and manage bookings through a structured workflow.

User Authentication

Secure login and account management functionality.

Admin Dashboard

Centralized management for bookings and customer records.

Service Control

Manage available services, schedules and availability.
